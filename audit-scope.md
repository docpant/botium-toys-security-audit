## Audit Scope

This audit evaluates the overall security posture of Botium Toys, a small business with a physical storefront, warehouse, and an expanding online presence. The scope includes all systems, assets, and processes managed by the IT department.

The audit covers:

- Employee devices such as desktops, laptops, and mobile devices  
- Internal network and internet access  
- E-commerce platform and online services  
- Data storage, retention, and internal databases  
- Payment processing systems handling customer financial data  
- Legacy systems requiring ongoing maintenance  
- Physical infrastructure, including office and warehouse security  

## Audit Goals

The objective of this audit is to assess current security controls, identify risks and vulnerabilities, and evaluate compliance with industry standards and regulations.

The audit focuses on:

- Identifying and classifying organizational assets  
- Evaluating existing security controls  
- Detecting gaps in protection, detection, and response mechanisms  
- Assessing compliance with relevant standards such as PCI DSS and GDPR  

The goal is to provide recommendations to improve security, reduce risks, and support secure business operations as the company continues to grow.
